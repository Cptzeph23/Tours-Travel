Who Are We?

We are a Travel agency. Helping you tour around Kenya.

UniQuest was born from the dream of a young girl who believed the world was full of untold stories waiting to be discovered. That dream grew into a passion — to connect people, culture, and nature through meaningful travel experiences.
At UniQuest, we don’t just plan tours; we curate journeys that leave footprints on both hearts and horizons. Every trip is designed to celebrate authenticity — blending adventure, culture, and comfort in perfect harmony.
We believe travel should not only show you new places but also help you feel them — their rhythm, their people, and their hidden beauty.
